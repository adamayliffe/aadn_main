Amy's Angels Day Nursery provides a variety of services up to the possible highest standards, as we understand the importance of your child's/children's fundamental years of their education which sets your child's/children's educational path in life. As well as supporting and encouraging them in their developmental areas in a nurturing and safe environment for their personalities to flourish as well as giving them the independence to make safe decisions and create friendship groups within the setting. We are not only here to support your child/children on their developmental journey at the nursery, We also pride ourselves on our parent and partners (parent and staff relationship). This means that you can come to a staff member with complete trust and confidentiality with any issues regarding you and your child/children with any extra support on advice that you may need or want.
